NeuroSync

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What is NeuroSync?

NeuroSync is a wellbeing app designed to help couples — including those with ADHD, autism, AuDHD, or other neurodivergent profiles — stay in tune with each other's mental states, energy levels, and needs. It is not a medical app and is not a substitute for professional mental health support.

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to all users of the NeuroSync mobile app on iOS and Android. NeuroSync is not intended for anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below and we will delete it.

Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address and display name (required to create an account)
  • Profile photo (optional, uploaded by you)
  • Neurotype selection (e.g. ADHD, autism, AuDHD, neurotypical)

Check-in data

  • Mental state selections (e.g. "anxious", "overstimulated", custom states you create)
  • Physical and mental energy levels (numeric values, 1–10)
  • Current need selections (e.g. "quiet time", "reassurance", custom needs you create)
  • Timestamps of check-ins

This data is shared with your connected partner within the app. It is not used for advertising or sold to third parties.

Partnership data

  • Partnership ID and invite code used to connect with your partner
  • Subscription status (active / inactive) linked to your partnership

Device and notification data

  • Push notification tokens (used to send you check-in and partnership alerts)
  • Device platform (iOS or Android) for notification routing

Payment data

Subscription purchases are processed by RevenueCat and the relevant app store (Apple App Store or Google Play). We do not store your payment card details. RevenueCat provides us with subscription status and anonymised purchase history. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details of how they handle payment data.

How we use your information

  • To operate and maintain your NeuroSync account
  • To display your check-ins to your connected partner
  • To send push notifications when your partner checks in or joins your partnership
  • To filter app content (mental states, needs, educational content) based on your neurotype
  • To process and manage your subscription
  • To improve the app based on aggregate usage patterns

Third-party services we use

  • Supabase — secure cloud database where your account, check-in, and partnership data is stored
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and purchase verification
  • Firebase (Google) — push notification delivery (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication method

These providers are contractually required to protect your data and may not use it for their own marketing purposes.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your check-in data is shared only with your connected partner within the app. We do not share personal data with advertisers. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

App permissions

  • Photo library — to let you choose a profile photo. Only used when you actively choose a photo.
  • Push notifications — to alert you when your partner checks in or joins. You can disable this in your device settings at any time.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and check-in history will be deleted within 30 days. Partnership data may be retained in anonymised form for aggregate analysis.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

Data deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

Email reesehayman@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We use row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data and their connected partner's check-in data. We take reasonable steps to protect against unauthorised access, but no system is completely secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you within the app. Continued use of NeuroSync after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
