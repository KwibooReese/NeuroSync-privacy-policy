Privacy Policy for NeuroSync

Last updated: January 14, 2026

About NeuroSync

NeuroSync is a communication app designed to help neurodivergent and neurotypical partners, roommates, and family members better understand and support each other. Our app helps users share mental states, needs, and energy levels while providing educational resources about ADHD, autism, emotional dysregulation, and overstimulation.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, profile information, and account preferences
  • Wellness Check-ins: Mental state updates, energy levels, needs, and feelings you choose to share
  • Profile Photos: Images you upload for your profile
  • Communication Data: Messages and information shared within the app between connected users

Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device information and technical data necessary for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the NeuroSync service
  • To enable communication between connected users (partners, family members, roommates)
  • To display your wellness check-ins to your connected contacts
  • To provide personalized educational content about neurodivergence
  • To improve our app and develop new features
  • To process subscription payments through our payment provider

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database service. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Connected Users: Wellness check-ins and communication data you choose to share with your partners, family members, or roommates
  • Service Providers: With trusted service providers like Supabase (data storage) and RevenueCat (payment processing) who help us operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

Permissions

Camera Access

NeuroSync requests camera permission to allow you to take photos for your profile picture. Photos are stored securely and are only shared with your connected contacts if you choose to include them in your profile.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your account information
  • Control what information you share with connected users
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Children's Privacy

NeuroSync is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
